About Publications Misc.

Hi! I am Xiaoya Li, a graduate student at the University of Washington. Before that, I was lucky to spend a few years at AI startups, working with Jiwei Li, Tianwei Zhang, and Guoyin Wang. I received my B.Eng. from BUPT. During my undergraduate studies, I was fortunate to work with Minlie Huang, Xiaojie Wang, Zhidong Deng and Zhengdong Lu. I have learned a lot from many people along the way, including research and beyond.

I work on Natural Language Processing and Machine Learning.
